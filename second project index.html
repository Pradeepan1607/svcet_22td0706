Ingredient

Mutton RIP Bone Soup

  • Oil - 1 Tsp
  • Cinnamon
  • Clove
  • Fennel seeds - 1/2 Tsp
  • Bay leaf
  • Mutton Bone/Nenju Elumbu - 250 gms
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Tomato - 1 no.
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 Tsp
  • Rock Salt
  • Curry leaves
  • Water - 3 cups
  • Coriander leaves
  • For masala powder
  • Cumin seeds - 1 Tsp
  • Cinnamon
  • Peppercorn - 1 Tsp Coriander seeds - 1

    • NOTE:This All the ingredients are change as per your liking

Methods

In a mixer jar, add cumin seeds, peppercorns, coriander seeds Grind it to a fine powder Pound some garlic with the skin and keep it aside Pound some shallots roughly and keep it aside In a pressure cooker, heat some oil, add cinnamon, cloves, fennel seeds, bay leaf, mutton rib bones, pound garlic, pound shallots Saute them Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, ground masala powder. rock salt, curry leaves Mix everything well Add 250 ml of water and mix well Close the pressure cooker and cook it for about 30 minutes Finally, open the pressure cooker and add coriander leaves and curry leaves Serve the soup hot with a dash of pepper
